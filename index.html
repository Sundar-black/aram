Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your homeopathic journey.

Is homeopathy safe for children and pregnant women? +

Yes, homeopathic medicines are completely safe for all ages including newborns, children, and pregnant women. Our remedies are highly diluted, non-toxic, and free from any harmful chemicals or side effects, making them ideal for sensitive populations.

How long does homeopathic treatment take to show results? +

This depends on the nature and duration of the condition. Acute conditions often respond within days. Chronic diseases that have persisted for years may take several months of consistent treatment. Most patients notice improvement within the first 4–8 weeks.

Can I take homeopathic medicines alongside allopathic treatment? +

In most cases, yes. Our doctors can guide you on combining treatments safely. For chronic conditions, we typically work toward gradually reducing dependency on conventional medicines as your health improves — always under proper medical supervision.

How does the online consultation work? +

You can book an appointment through our website or WhatsApp. The doctor will connect with you via video or audio call at your scheduled time. After a detailed case study, your personalized medicines will be dispatched to your address, wherever you are in India.

Do I need to follow any diet during treatment? +

Yes, certain foods and substances can interfere with homeopathic remedies. Our doctors will give you specific dietary guidance based on your treatment. Generally, we advise avoiding strong-smelling substances like camphor and raw onion within 30 minutes of taking medicines.

What are your consultation charges? +

Our consultation fees are very reasonable and affordable. We offer flexible payment options. Please contact us on WhatsApp or call us directly to get current fee details, as we customize plans based on the treatment duration and type of condition.
